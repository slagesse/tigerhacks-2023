Purpose

The United States faces a crisis in news media — while traditional sources of journalistic revenue decline, Americans are less trusting than ever of the major news publications crucial to our democracy.
Despite this, there remains a lack of efficient and simple methods for the general public to voice their opinions about the news: most publications' only source of contact with their viewership are sparse qualitative data through janky comment systems.

What is RedPen?

Deriving its name from the "red pen" with which editors critique the first draft of a news story, RedPen is a survey-like Chrome extension and web app that allows for the general public to directly submit feedback to news publications on individual pieces of content.The publications can then use the feedback to improve their reporting and connect with their audience, simultaneously bolstering news quality while reforming critical trust within a heavily polarized society.

How does it work?

Members of the general public can create a user account, which will allow them to submit reviews through the RedPen Review Chrome extension. Publications then use that data!
(The chrome extension is still under review, but can be seen in the demo)
